Our Closing Process

  • 01

    Got a New Deal?

    Kick things off by clicking “Contact Us” and filling out our brief onboarding form.

  • 02

    Deal Onboarding

    Once submitted, we’ll bring your transaction into our system, collect earnest money, and draft your escrow agreement.

  • 03

    Getting Ready to Close

    Submit your Closing Questionnaire at least 7 days before your target closing date (the sooner, the better!). This gives us time to prepare and circulate key closing documents — including the bill of sale, promissory note, and more — so everything’s ready before closing day.

  • 04

    Final Prep

    We’ll run a lien search, request property tax info from the county, and draft all required closing documents for your deal.

  • 05

    Time to Close

    Once everything is approved, we’ll coordinate a smooth closing — either in person or electronically — with all involved parties: Buyer, Seller, Broker, Lender, and more.
